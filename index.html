Our Story

Merchant services with better structure and stronger clarity.

Zing Card Payment Systems provides businesses with up to date pricing and information for processing all types of credit cards and debit cards. We want to help you better understand what other credit card processing companies are doing and get you the lowest rates on your service.

Understanding the basics of the merchant service industry is very important when selecting a processing provider. Zing Card is committed to giving our clients the most cost effective ways to grow their business.

Visa and MasterCard have over 300 different charge types that can affect your costs. We will show you how certain card types and the methods of accepting those cards can cause your rates to fluctuate.

Credit cards are impacting your net profit, a crucial element to your bottom line. Understand the difference in pricing structures and what it means to your effective rate.

It is free to get a quote. It is important to make sure you are receiving the lowest rates possible. The merchant services industry is always changing - we will help you lower your current rates.

Zing Zero Pay Program

Zero cost processing, reframed as a premium program page.

Zero cost on credit cards. Simple, transparent pricing. There is a solution for every way you accept cards: Face-to-Face, POS, Web, Virtual Terminal.

Bottom line is you pay zero on all credit card transactions. We do not need to look at your statement, just look at your credit card costs and that is what you will save.

  • Zing Pay allows customers to pay the fee if they choose a credit card to pay. If they use a debit card the business pays the fee. Keep in mind debit fees are super low.
  • When customers choose a debit card, they pay no fee, keeping you compliant and giving your customers a choice.
  • Your customers always have a no-fee payment option. To comply with the rules, we automatically detect when a debit card is entered and apply no fee.
  • You pay only the low cost of debit card acceptance.
  • Fully compliant: our turnkey solution complies with all the rules and automatically has you covered.
  • Customer friendly: your customers can always choose debit as a no-fee option.
  • Zero cost: you keep 100% of every credit card sale; when you sell $100, you receive $100. Pay only for debit card transactions.

How to Sign-up?

  • Fill out merchant application to setup an account.
  • We fill out required paperwork to insure compliancy with the card associations.
  • Receive Custom Terminal / Website / Mobile app with EMV Quick Chip and the ZC application pre-installed. Plug in the terminal or setup our POS and you are ready to go.
